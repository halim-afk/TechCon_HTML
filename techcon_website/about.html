Our History

Since its launch in 2010, TechCon has grown from a small meetup of passionate developers to one of the world's most respected tech conferences. Each year, it brings together thought leaders and pioneers from around the globe.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower and connect the global tech community. We strive to provide a platform for sharing innovation, advancing open-source collaboration, and promoting diversity in technology.
